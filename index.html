Tawang sightseeing highlights

Popular Places To Visit

Explore the breathtaking beauty of Tawang

Border route

Bumla Pass

Experience snow-covered mountains and breathtaking Indo-China border views. This high-altitude route is one of the most requested Tawang day trips and is best planned with local drivers who know permit checks, road conditions, and weather windows.

Sacred falls

108 Holy Waterfalls

A magical collection of sacred waterfalls surrounded by pure nature. The route is loved by travelers who want quiet landscapes, fresh mountain air, and meaningful stops away from crowded viewpoints.

Alpine lake

Shongatser Lake

Famous as Madhuri Lake, known for its peaceful and scenic beauty. The lake is a favorite stop for photography, calm walks, and slow travel through the dramatic highland landscape around Tawang.

Offbeat valley

Mago & Chuna Valley

Discover untouched valleys, snow landscapes, and adventurous roads. These routes reward patient travelers with remote scenery and a deeper look at the natural side of Arunachal Pradesh.

Quiet lake

Gongkharla Lake

Hidden Himalayan lake offering stunning reflections and peaceful vibes. It is ideal for travelers who prefer quieter stops and want to add a less-commercial lake experience to their Tawang itinerary.

Heritage stop

Iron Bridge (Chakzam)

Historic bridge showcasing ancient engineering and cultural heritage. Chakzam is a meaningful stop for visitors interested in local history, old trade routes, and the cultural depth of the Tawang region.

Cultural valley

Zimithang

Peaceful valley destination rich with natural beauty and culture. The route is well suited for travelers who enjoy quiet villages, open landscapes, and scenic drives through the far edges of Tawang district.
